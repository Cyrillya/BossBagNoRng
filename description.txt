这个Mod优化了原版所有宝藏袋与Boss宠物/坐骑物品的物品掉率，以让你不需要重复击杀某个Boss来获取他的某个战利品
以下是该Mod所作调整的列表:

Boss宠物/坐骑:
- 几率调整为100%
所有宝藏袋:
- 必开出相应Boss的纪念章
史莱姆王宝藏袋:
- 粘鞍几率调整为100%
- 忍者套一次开出全套
克苏鲁之眼宝藏袋:
- 猩红矿/魔矿必开出最大数量(87个)
独眼巨鹿宝藏袋:
- 眼骨、眼球伞、收音机将一次性全部开出
- 以上物品会额外随机附加一个 (为了跨模组兼容性)
血肉墙宝藏袋:
- 四徽章将一次性全部开出
- 四徽章会额外随机附加一个 (为了跨模组兼容性) (说的就是你灾厄)
史莱姆皇后宝藏袋:
- 明胶女式鞍几率调整为100%
- 失谐钩爪几率调整为100%
- 水晶刺客套一次开出全套
机械三王宝藏袋:
- 神圣锭必开出最大数量(35个)
- 相应魂必开出最大数量(40个)
世纪之花宝藏袋:
- 刺钩几率调整为100%
石巨人宝藏袋:
- 锯刃镐几率调整为100%
- 甲虫外壳必开出最大数量(23个)
猪龙鱼公爵宝藏袋:
- 猪龙鱼之翼几率调整为100%
光之女皇宝藏袋:
- 女皇之翼几率调整为100%
双足翼龙宝藏袋:
- 双足翼龙之翼几率调整为100%
- 护卫奖章必开出最大数量(49个)

This mod optimizes the drop rate of the items in the vanilla treasure bags and master pets and mounts, so you don't have to kill a boss over and over again to get a single loot
Here is the list of adjustments made by the mod:

Master pets and mounts from bosses:
- Drop rate adjusted to 100%
All treasure bags:
- Includes the trophy of the corresponding boss
King Slime Treasure Bag:
- Slimy Saddle drop rate adjusted to 100%
- Ninja set will be dropped all at once
Eye of Cthulhu Treasure Bag:
- The most crimtane/demonite ores will be dropped (87)
Deerclops Treasure Bag:
- Eye Bone, Eyebrella and Radio Thing will be dropped all at once
- The above items will be randomly attached to an additional one (for cross-mod compatibility)
Wall of Flesh Treasure Bag:
- All four emblems will be dropped all at once
- Emblems will be randomly attached to an additional one (for cross-mod compatibility) (especially for calamity)
Queen Slime Treasure Bag:
- Gelatinous Pillion drop rate adjusted to 100%
- Hook of Dissonance drop rate adjusted to 100%
- Crystal Assassin set will be dropped all at once
Mechanical Bosses Treasure Bags:
- The most hallowed bars will be dropped (35)
- The most souls will be dropped (40 for all)
Plantera Treasure Bag:
- Thorn Hook drop rate adjusted to 100%
Golem Treasure Bag:
- Picksaw drop rate adjusted to 100%
- The most beetle husks will be dropped (23)
Duke Fishron Treasure Bag:
- Fishron Wings drop rate adjusted to 100%
Empress of Light Treasure Bag:
- Empress Wings drop rate adjusted to 100%
Betsy Treasure Bag:
- Betsy's Wings drop rate adjusted to 100%
- The most defender medals will be dropped (49)
